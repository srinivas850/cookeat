Ingredients

  1. Rajma (kidney beans)
  2. Onions
  3. Tomatoes
  4. Ginger
  5. Garlic
  6. Green chilies
  7. Cumin seeds
  8. Coriander powder
  9. Turmeric
  10. Red chili powder
  11. Garam masala
  12. Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  13. Salt
  14. Oil
  15. Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Preparation

  1. Cook the Rajma: Soak kidney beans overnight, then pressure cook with salt until soft and tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Gravy: Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions, ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Saut√© until onions are golden brown. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy. Mash the tomatoes with the back of a spoon.
  3. Add Spices: Add turmeric, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt to the tomato-onion mixture. Cook until the spices are fragrant and oil starts to separate from the masala.
  4. Simmer with Rajma: Add the cooked rajma to the masala along with some water. Simmer on low heat for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the curry to thicken.
  5. Finish and Serve: Crush kasuri methi between your palms and sprinkle over the rajma curry. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving hot with steamed rice or roti.
