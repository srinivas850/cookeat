Ingredients

  1. 500g boneless chicken pieces
  2. 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  3. 2 tbsp yogurt
  4. 1 tbsp lemon juice
  5. 1 tsp red chili powder
  6. 1 tsp turmeric powder
  7. 1 tsp garam masala
  8. 1 tsp cumin powder
  9. 1 tsp coriander powder
  10. Salt to taste
  11. Oil for deep frying
  12. Curry leaves for garnish
  13. Green chilies for garnish

Steps for Chicken 65

  1. Marinate the chicken pieces with ginger-garlic paste, yogurt, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
  3. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated chicken pieces in batches and fry until they are golden brown and cooked through. Drain excess oil on paper towels.
  4. In a separate pan, heat a little oil and fry curry leaves and green chilies for garnish until they become crisp.
  5. Serve the Chicken 65 hot, garnished with fried curry leaves and green chilies. Enjoy your delicious Chicken 65!
