Ingredients

  1. Basmati rice
  2. Chicken or mutton
  3. Onions
  4. Tomatoes
  5. Yogurt
  6. Ginger-garlic paste
  7. Garam masala
  8. Turmeric
  9. Cumin
  10. Coriander
  11. Chili powder
  12. Saffron
  13. Mint leaves
  14. Cilantro
  15. Salt
  16. Ghee/oil

Preparation

  1. Parboil Rice: Rinse basmati rice until the water runs clear. Parboil the rice in salted water until it's 70-80% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  2. Marinate the Meat: Marinate chicken or mutton pieces in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, and salt for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Layering: In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer the parboiled rice and marinated meat. Add fried onions, saffron-infused milk, mint leaves, and cilantro between the layers. Seal the pot with dough or a tight-fitting lid.
  4. Cooking: Place the sealed pot on low heat and cook for about 30-40 minutes, or until the rice and meat are fully cooked and aromatic.
  5. Serve: Gently fluff the biryani with a fork before serving hot with raita (yogurt dip) and salad.
