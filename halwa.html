Ingredients

  1. 6-8 slices of bread (white or brown)
  2. 1 cup sugar
  3. 1 cup milk
  4. 4-5 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  5. A handful of mixed nuts (cashews, almonds, pistachios) - chopped
  6. 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  7. Saffron strands (optional)
  8. Water as needed

Preparation

  1. Trim the edges of the bread slices and cut them into small pieces.
  2. Heat the milk in a saucepan until it simmers. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. Crush the saffron strands (if using) and soak them in a tablespoon of warm milk.
