Ingredients

  1. Spinach
  2. Paneer (Indian cheese)
  3. Onions
  4. Tomatoes
  5. Ginger
  6. Garlic
  7. Garam masala
  8. Turmeric
  9. Cumin
  10. Coriander
  11. Chili powder
  12. Cream
  13. Butter
  14. Salt
  15. Oil

Preparation

  1. Prepare the Spinach Puree: Blanch spinach leaves in boiling water, then immediately transfer them to ice water to retain their color. Blend the blanched spinach with green chilies to a smooth puree.
  2. Cook the Gravy: In a pan, heat oil and saut√© onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, garam masala, cumin, coriander, chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  3. Combine Spinach and Gravy: Add the spinach puree to the pan. Cook for a few minutes, then stir in cream and paneer cubes. Simmer until the paneer is heated through and the flavors meld together.
  4. Finish and Serve: Garnish with a swirl of cream and chopped cilantro before serving hot with rice or naan.
